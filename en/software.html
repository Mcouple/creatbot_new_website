CreatWare: Powerful 3D printer slicing software

Creatware creates personalized configurations for different 3D printers, consumables and models to facilitate rapid storage of configurations for later use. The above configuration files can also be exported and imported by other members of the team, so that the teams can share with each other in a simplified workflow.

Custom support structure

Using a fully customized support system, creatware can easily and automatically create accurate support structures or 3D prints. You can change the thickness of brackets, add or remove brackets manually.

Adaptive layer height - combination of high speed and high quality

CreatWare can automatically increase or decrease the layer height according to the required fineness of the current printing area, so as to further improve the printing quality and reduce the printing time.

Open Filament Project

CreatWare can use third-party consumables, which are classified by brand and material. Users can download and save them in the OFP directory. With ideamaker, tested and certified consumables in OFP will have preset configuration files in which we have preset the temperature and speed for you.
