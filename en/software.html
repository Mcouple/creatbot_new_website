BCN3D Stratos

BCN3D Stratos is a straightforward slicing software, designed for speed and efficiency while still ensuring top-quality prints for a powerful 3D printing process.
The user-friendly interface is free and compatible with all BCN3D Sigma and Epsilon printers.
